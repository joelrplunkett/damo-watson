About

BETWEEN CROISSANTS AND CLIMBS

Based in Muriwai, New Zealand, Damian Watson splits life between the bakery, the trail, and long stretches of European road. If he is not at the Muriwai Deli shaping dough, he is usually somewhere uphill telling himself the climb will be worth it later.

He is not much for polished athlete speeches. Better with dirt underfoot, something hard on the calendar, and a decent feed waiting at the end.

Why run this far?

Because moving fixes things.

Best medicine going. Plus, it earns the pud.
