About

BETWEEN CROISSANTS AND CLIMBS

Based in Muriwai, New Zealand, Damo splits his time between croissants, climbs, and long stretches of European road. If he’s not at the Muriwai Deli shaping dough, he’s somewhere uphill convincing himself the gradient is character-building.

He runs so he can shovel in the pud. He keeps running because stopping feels worse. The legs complain; something in his head calls it training.

Not big on finish-line speeches. Usually too busy finding food.
